    Some ideas for improving the multipamellar model (lamellar_hg_stack_caille) to better fit Sample2:
  • There may be polydispersity in the model (not all particles are the same). E.g. they may contain different number of layers (polydispersity in the parameter Nlayers).
  • The model contains no information about the shape of the vesicle (it only assumes that is contains many layers), and assumes that the vesicles are infinitely large (radius->inf) - maybe the finite vesicle radius could be build into the model?
  • The vesicles could be imaged, e.g. with electron microscopy, to get an independent estimation of its structure.
  • The overall size may be determined with, e.g. dynamic light scattering or other biophysical techniques.
